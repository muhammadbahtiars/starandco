Founded in 2004, Star & Co started as a small family paddock with just ten cows. Today, we've grown into a regional leader in sustainable dairy, but our philosophy hasn't changed.

We believe that happy cows produce better milk. That's why our animals enjoy stress-free environments, individual health monitoring, and thousands of acres of wild pasture.
