Common Inquiries

Are you organic certified?

Yes, we hold Soil Association certification. Our cattle are 100% pasture-raised and never treated with hormones.

Can we visit without booking?

To ensure the health of our herd, we require all farm visits to be booked 48 hours in advance via our portal.

Do you offer wholesale delivery?

We deliver to artisanal bakeries and fine dining establishments within a 50-mile radius of the Cotswolds.
