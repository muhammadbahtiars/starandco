Our Heritage

It began with a single Jersey cow and a devotion to the rhythm of the seasons. Our founders believed that the finest milk isn't manufactured—it's nurtured. For three generations, the Star family has protected the pastoral traditions of our valley.

We've resisted the industrial tide, choosing instead to maintain the artisanal methods that preserve the natural cream-top and complex nutrient profile of real, non-homogenized dairy.
